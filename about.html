Welcome To Brights Classes

Your Destination for a Bright Tomorrow

About Brights

Bright’s was established in 2000 on a foundation of belief, trust and care, with minimal infrastructure, supported by a friend and well-wishers who believed that the founder is meant for success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a high quality, comprehensive, and meaningful education for all students.

Our Vision

The vision of our academy is to leave no stone unturned to lead the present generation of children towards the path of achieving their dreams, as these very dreams are our own inspiration.

Our Founder

Mr. Jeans Paul , the founder of Bright’s Classes has the vision of building an education system that empowers students with knowledge, the desire to learn and self-discipline. He believes in leading by example every day.His upbringing in an environment of sheer adversity has given him humility, and a simple outlook towards life. As he believes, “in adversities, life provides solutions.”

Our Values

Service Excellence

We believe our high quality educational system; the teaching faculty expertise and the support materials that helps in delivering high quality service. We feel responsible towards delivering exceptional performance – a hallmark of Bright’s – demanding of ourselves and each other because our work impacts student's academic orientation. We believe that when a student is surrounded by superior teachers, it lifts up their academic performance and helps in overall growth.

Integrity

We commit to maintain the highest ethical and quality standards without compromise.

Humanity

Our actions reflect on others; hence, wellbeing of our students remains our core. We strive to bring about a sustainable impact on education and learning by providing a helping hand to those in need and highly deserve
